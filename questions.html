Frequently Asked Questions:

1. How are the points divided?▼

The points are divided by the regular F1 score rules: 1. 26p 2. 18p 3. 15p 4. 12p 5. 10p 6. 8p 7. 6p 8. 4p 9. 2p 10. 1p, but in the f1 fantasy we have som extra points to give out: Pole position: 3p, Fastest lap 3p.

2. What is the penalty for having more than one driver from the same team?▼

The penalty for having more than one driver from the same team is -10p per round.

3. How do I join f1 fantasy?▼

To join or make changes to your team you have to look for "Fantasy" on the website where you press "create".

4. When can i change my team?▼

You can change your team before each race weekend. However, once the deadline for making changes passes(Qualification), your lineup will be locked in for that race.

5. When will I see my score?▼

Your team’s score will update throughout the weekend. A provisional score will show shortly after the conclusion of qualifying, the Sprint and the Grand Prix. Your final score will be calculated as soon as all the data has come back from the track and scoring events (like overtakes and fastest pitstops) are validated. This might be a few hours after the chequered flag has been waved.

6. Is it free to play?▼

Yes, it’s free to play!

7. What is the budget for my f1 fantasy team?▼

You have $100M in budget to pick four drivers! Reed more about the drivers value under "Drivers".

8. What is the deadline for making changes to my team?▼

The deadline for making changes to your team lineup is typically before the start of the qualifying session for each race.

9. How do i ask questions that i cant't find on the website?▼

By clicking the support/question button you can ask a question of your own!

10. What happens if my chosen driver gets replaced?▼

If your chosen driver gets replaced before a race, you will not earn points for that race unless you make a substitution before the deadline. Look at the News section to reed about the drivers and more...
