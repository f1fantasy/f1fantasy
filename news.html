F1 2024 News:

Carlos Sainz out for Australia!?

Carlos Sainz's appendix surgery jeopardizes his presence at the Australian Grand Prix. Young talent Ollie Bearman could step in, injecting excitement into Ferrari's decision.

Toto Wolff wants Verstappen!

Toto Wolff has admitted that he would “love” to have Max Verstappen at Mercedes should the Dutchman become available, though the team boss also acknowledged that the current priority for the Silver Arrows is to improve their car before thinking too much about the future.

Tsunoda "take the next step"!

RB boss Laurent Mekies admits that the team are looking for Yuki Tsunoda to take the “next step” in 2024 to prove that he is continuing to develop all-round as a driver, with team mate Daniel Ricciardo acting as a “fantastic reference point”.
